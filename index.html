5:2 Fasting Planner
What is 5:2?
Eat normally 5 days a week. On 2 chosen days, restrict calories to 500 kcal (men) or 400 kcal (women). Research shows it's as effective as continuous calorie restriction, with better adherence for many people.
Choose Your 2 Fast Days
💡 Non-consecutive days work best — e.g. Monday & Thursday

Your 89kg → 82kg Plan
🎯 At 89kg your TDEE is ~2,300 kcal/day. With 5:2 you create a weekly deficit of ~3,600 kcal — enough for 0.5–0.7 kg/week loss.
⏱️ At this rate, reaching 82kg should take 10–14 weeks. Consistency beats perfection.
💧 Fast day essentials: water, black coffee, herbal tea. One 400 kcal high-protein meal, or split 200+300. Eat late to minimise hunger window.
🏃 Keep exercise light on fast days — walks are ideal. Save intense training for normal days when you're properly fuelled.
